# Corporate Compliance Tooling

**Track:** [[legal]]

## Description

SaaS and workflow tooling for German law firms (Kanzleien) and in-house legal teams navigating the mandatory digitalisation of court proceedings. Three regulatory deadlines have converged: eAkte (electronic court file) became mandatory for all civil and commercial courts on 1 January 2026; the Online-Zivilverfahren pilot for claims up to €10,000 launched on 15 April 2026 across eight Amtsgerichte; and the beA (besonderes elektronisches Anwaltspostfach) has been mandatory since 2022. Legacy case-management systems (RA-MICRO, Advoware, STP) do not interoperate natively with these platforms, creating integration and workflow gaps that fall on law firms to resolve.

## Key Regulations

| Regulation | What it requires | In force |
|---|---|---|
| eAkte mandate | All civil and commercial court files must be electronic | 1 January 2026 |
| beA obligation | All admitted lawyers must use the secure lawyer mailbox | Since 2022 |
| Online-Zivilverfahren | Digital small-claims process for cases up to €10,000 | 15 April 2026 (pilot) |
| E-Justice Strategie 2027 | Full interoperability across Bundesländer court systems | 2027 target |
| ERVV (Elektronischer Rechtsverkehr) | Format and transmission standards for electronic court filings | Ongoing |

## Market Size

- ~165,000 admitted lawyers in Germany; ~50,000 in 3–50 person Kanzleien.
- At €60/lawyer/month average and 15% penetration: SAM €20–40m ARR within 3 years.
- Adjacent: ~10,000 in-house legal teams at German corporates facing the same eAkte obligations.

## Competitive Landscape

| Player | Origin | Positioning | Gap |
|---|---|---|---|
| RA-MICRO | DE | Market-leading Kanzlei CMS | Slow to adopt eAkte and Online-Zivilverfahren integrations |
| STP / Advolux / Advoware | DE | CMS vendors | Fragmented, Land-specific, no cross-system interoperability layer |
| Legartis / XMLaw | DE | Contract intelligence | Not court-filing focused |
| Kleos (Clio) | EU | Cloud Kanzlei management | Partial German coverage, no eAkte native |
| Smokeball | AU/US | Small-firm practice management | Not localised for German court systems |

## Best-in-Class Benchmarks

- **Clio (Themis Solutions, CA)** — benchmark for cloud-native law practice management UX and integrations.
- **NetDocuments** — gold standard for document management and court-filing workflow in large firms.
- **Smokeball** — reference for time-capture automation and SME law firm adoption.
