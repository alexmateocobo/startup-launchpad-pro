# Digital Accessibility and Compliance

**Track:** [[legal]]

## Description

Tooling for companies that must prove continuous conformance with the Barrierefreiheitsstärkungsgesetz (BFSG) and the underlying technical standards WCAG 2.2 AA and EN 301 549. The market is driven by the BFSG enforcement date of 28 June 2025 — Germany's transposition of the EU Accessibility Act — which applies to all B2C digital products and services. Enforcement sits with 15 Länder Marktüberwachungsbehörden with fine authority up to €100,000 and market-removal powers. A 2025 industry audit found zero fully compliant sites among 115 major German online shops, signalling a large, immediate compliance gap.

## Key Regulations

| Regulation | What it requires | In force |
|---|---|---|
| BFSG (Barrierefreiheitsstärkungsgesetz) | WCAG 2.2 AA + EN 301 549 for all B2C digital products and services | 28 June 2025 |
| EU Accessibility Act (EAA) | Parent directive transposed by BFSG | 2025 |
| EN 301 549 | European harmonised accessibility standard for ICT | Ongoing |
| WCAG 2.2 | W3C technical criteria referenced by EN 301 549 | Ongoing |
| BFSG-V (Verordnung) | Implementing regulation specifying documentation and reporting obligations | 2025 |

## Market Size

- ~500,000 B2C digital products and services in Germany subject to BFSG.
- Of which ~70,000 are Mittelstand or large enterprises with meaningful budget.
- At €15k average ACV and 5% penetration: SAM ~€50m; TAM €200–400m including EU transpositions.

## Best-in-Class Benchmarks

- **Deque / axe-core** sets the technical benchmark for automated WCAG rule coverage (~57% of criteria automatable).
- **Siteimprove** is the reference for enterprise continuous-monitoring UX.
- **AbilityNet (UK)** and **WebAIM** demonstrate the lived-experience audit model that regulators treat as gold standard.
