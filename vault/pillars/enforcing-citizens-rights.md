# Enforcing Citizens' Rights

**Track:** [[legal]]

## Description

Infrastructure for enforcing individual rights at scale — primarily GDPR Article 82 non-material damages claims, whistleblowing obligations under HinSchG, and access to justice tools for citizens who face systemic rights violations. The structural opportunity is that enforcement is bottlenecked by claim quality and evidence, not by the volume of potential claimants. The CJEU and BGH have sharpened the "concrete harm" threshold for GDPR damages (2025–2026 rulings), creating a filtering and triage gap that plaintiff Kanzleien and litigation funders cannot fill manually. Separately, the HinSchG (Hinweisgeberschutzgesetz) requires companies with 50+ employees to operate internal reporting channels — creating a compliance market for whistleblower management software.

## Key Regulations

| Regulation | What it requires | In force |
|---|---|---|
| GDPR Article 82 | Right to compensation for material and non-material damages from data breaches | Since 2018 |
| CJEU / BGH "concrete harm" doctrine | Claimants must demonstrate a specific, well-founded fear of misuse | Established 2025–2026 |
| HinSchG (Hinweisgeberschutzgesetz) | Internal and external whistleblowing channels for companies with 50+ employees | Since 2 July 2023 |
| VDuG (Verbandsklagenrichtlinie) | Class-action mechanism for qualified entities (Abhilfeklage) | Since 2023 |
| EU Product Liability Directive 2024/2853 | Extends burden of proof rules to digital and AI-caused harm | 2024 |
| Digital Fairness Act | Strengthens consumer digital rights; expected to add new claim paths | Expected 2026 |

## Market Size

- GDPR Article 82: Meta, TikTok, and X Verbandsklagen touch tens of millions of German data subjects; even 1% attach rate is 100k+ cases.
- Kanzlei and litigation funder TAM for data-breach claim infrastructure in Germany: €30–80m.
- HinSchG compliance SaaS: ~700,000 German companies with 50+ employees in scope; market at €500–2,000/company/year.

## Best-in-Class Benchmarks

- **WhistleB / NAVEX Global** — benchmark for whistleblowing channel UX and audit-trail completeness.
- **Deminor** — sets the standard for litigation-funder-grade pre-screening documentation.
- **vzbv (Verbraucherzentrale Bundesverband)** — the reference qualified entity for Abhilfeklage scope and evidence standards.
