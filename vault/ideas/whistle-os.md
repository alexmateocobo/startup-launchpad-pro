# WhistleOS

**Pillar:** [[corporate-compliance-tooling]]
**One-liner:** Next-generation Hinweisgeberschutzgesetz (HinSchG) whistleblowing platform with AI-triage, case-management and integration with EU Whistleblower Directive, built for Mittelstand and large enterprises underserved by legacy providers.
**Shortlist score:** 28/35

## Problem

The HinSchG has been in force since 2 July 2023 and requires all German companies with 50+ employees to operate an internal reporting channel. Enforcement and inspection by the Bundesamt für Justiz intensified in 2024-2025. Incumbent platforms (EQS Compliance COCKPIT, LegalTegrity, Hintbox, Business Keeper) have a legacy user experience, brittle case-management, and thin AI support: triage and pattern detection are manual. Mid-size and medium-large Mittelstand companies (200-5,000 employees) report 50%+ cost overruns compared to expectation, poor reporter experience, and under-utilised internal reporting flow. Meanwhile new obligations keep arriving: CSRD non-financial reporting now requires disclosure of whistleblowing programme effectiveness; the EU AI Act requires reporting channels for impacted persons.

## Regulatory driver / Why now

- HinSchG operationalisation + Bundesamt für Justiz supervision.
- CSRD ESRS G1 disclosures on whistleblowing effectiveness from 2026.
- EU AI Act Article 85-86 requires a reporting mechanism for affected persons.
- DORA: whistleblowing is part of operational-resilience governance.
- First wave of Mittelstand reviewing 2023 vendor choice and looking to switch.

## Target users

- **End users:** compliance officers, ombudspersons, internal auditors.
- **Payers:**
  - Mittelstand (200-5,000 FTE): €8-40k/year.
  - Large enterprise: €75-250k/year.
  - Inhouse-Kanzleien and external ombudspersons who run shared intake.

## Solution

An AI-native whistleblowing platform:

1. Reporter UX: multi-language, anonymous or pseudonymous, voice-to-text transcription, guided-questioning with AI.
2. AI triage: classify report by severity, subject area, applicable regulation, relevant evidence.
3. Case workbench: parallel-track investigation, secure evidence store, interview scheduling, auto-drafted findings.
4. Cross-case analytics: pattern detection, hotspot maps, disclosure metrics ready for CSRD ESRS G1.
5. Integration with HR, ERP, SIEM for evidence.

## Business model

- **Tiered SaaS:** €6-30k/year Mittelstand, €75-250k/year enterprise.
- **Managed-service tier:** add ombudsperson on demand (€12-60k/year).
- **CSRD add-on:** €15-40k/year for analytics exports.

## Competitive landscape

- **EQS Compliance COCKPIT:** DAX brand but legacy UX.

- **Business Keeper by SAP:** enterprise but stagnating.

## Moat and differentiation

- Truly AI-native triage vs. legacy rule engines.
- CSRD-linked analytics makes whistleblowing a governance KPI, not a cost centre.
- EU Whistleblower Directive multi-language compliance.
- Integrations library (HR, ERP, SIEM).

## Regulatory path (RDG)

- Mittelstand price pressure.
