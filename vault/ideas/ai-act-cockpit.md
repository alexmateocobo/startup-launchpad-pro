# AI Act Cockpit

**Pillar:** [[corporate-compliance-tooling]]
**One-liner:** EU AI Act compliance cockpit that combines AI-system inventory, Annex III risk classification, technical documentation, post-market monitoring and conformity-assessment support, powered by process telemetry from existing enterprise systems (ERP, CRM, HRIS).
**Shortlist score:** 31/35

## Problem

The EU AI Act enters its main application phase on 2 August 2026: all High-Risk AI systems must meet conformity-assessment, technical-documentation, risk-management and post-market-monitoring requirements. German enforcement is governed by the KI-Marktüberwachungsgesetz (KI-MIG), delayed but expected 2026. Enterprises have hundreds to thousands of AI use cases embedded in ERP, CRM, HRIS, marketing tools, RPA bots. Nobody has a reliable inventory; classification (Annex III) is lawyer-time-heavy; technical documentation is scattered; post-market monitoring is aspirational. Existing tooling (Credo AI, Holistic AI, Lumenova, Trustible) targets US tech enterprises; few are tuned to DACH process-heavy Mittelstand and its integration with ERP/HRIS systems.

## Regulatory driver / Why now

- AI Act main deadline 2 August 2026.
- German KI-Marktüberwachungsgesetz in transposition.
- Notified Bodies (Akkreditierungsstellen for conformity assessment) being designated in 2026.
- DIN SPEC 92001-2, ISO/IEC 42001 standards operationalised.
- Bundesbank, BaFin, BNetzA issuing sectoral AI guidance.

## Target users

- **End users:** Chief AI Officers, CISOs, compliance, risk, and internal audit.
- **Payers:**
  - Financial services (especially BaFin-supervised).
  - Manufacturing, automotive, pharma, energy.
  - Retail, insurance, telco.
  - Public sector at federal and Länder level.

## Solution

1. **AI inventory engine:** connects to ERP, HR, CRM, MLOps, SaaS procurement to auto-discover AI systems.
2. **Risk classification:** guided questionnaires mapped to Annex III + national law, producing classification attestation.
3. **Technical documentation builder:** auto-generates Art. 11 documentation from model-card inputs + process telemetry.
4. **Post-market monitoring:** metrics pipeline (drift, bias, accuracy) and incident reporting (Art. 62).
5. **Conformity-assessment workflow:** connects to Notified Bodies.

Integration with process-mining platforms (Celonis, UiPath ProcessMaker) gives unique visibility into how AI systems actually operate inside processes — a natural fit for Alejandro's Celonis expertise.
